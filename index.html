Understanding Time-Value-Money

Present Value (PV)

The current worth of a future amount of money, considering a specific rate of return.

Future Value (FV)

The value of an investment after earning interest or returns over a set period.

Effective Annual Rate (EAT)

A measure of interest accounting for compounding, calculated annually.

Rule of 72

A simple formula to estimate how long it will take for an investment to double.
